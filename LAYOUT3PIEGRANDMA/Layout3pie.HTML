Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 6 medium-sized apples, peeled and sliced

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Toss the apples in the sugar cinnamon mixture.
  3. Place the apples in a pie dish and bake for 45 minutes.
